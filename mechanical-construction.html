OVERVIEW

Mechanical Construction, Fabrication & Maintenance

EDY-BO International provides expert mechanical construction, fabrication, and facility maintenance services for oil and gas infrastructure. We design, fabricate, install, and maintain process equipment, piping systems, and mechanical installations to the highest industry standards.

Our fabrication facility is equipped with modern equipment for structural steel fabrication, piping fabrication, and equipment skid assembly. We work to engineering specifications and industry codes, ensuring that all fabricated items meet quality and safety requirements before installation.

Beyond fabrication, we provide comprehensive mechanical installation services including equipment rigging, piping installation, vessel installation, and system commissioning. Our installation teams are experienced in working within operating facilities, minimizing disruption while maintaining safety.

Our facility maintenance services keep mechanical systems operating reliably. From rotating equipment maintenance to piping system repairs, from valve maintenance to emergency breakdown services, we provide the mechanical expertise that keeps facilities running.
