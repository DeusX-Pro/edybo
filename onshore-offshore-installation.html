OVERVIEW

Onshore & Offshore Construction Services

EDY-BO International delivers comprehensive construction and installation services for both onshore and offshore oil and gas facilities. From platform installations to pipeline laying, we execute complex projects with precision, safety, and adherence to strict offshore protocols.

Our onshore capabilities cover facility construction, equipment installation, and infrastructure development across Delta State's oil-producing areas. We understand the challenges of working in Niger Delta terrain and have the equipment and expertise to deliver projects in challenging environments.

For offshore operations, we provide specialized services including platform support, subsea equipment installation, marine construction, and offshore facility maintenance. Our teams are trained in offshore safety requirements and work procedures specific to marine environments.

Whether building new facilities, expanding existing operations, or installing critical equipment, EDY-BO International combines technical expertise with rigorous safety management to deliver successful onshore and offshore projects across Nigeria's oil and gas sector.
