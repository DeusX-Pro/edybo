FEATURED PROJECT

Second Niger Bridge Access Road

EDY-BO International provided essential construction support services for the Second Niger Bridge access road project linking Delta and Anambra States. The 17.5km dual carriage access road connects communities including Issele-Azagba, Okpanam, Ibusa, and Asaba to this landmark infrastructure that's transforming transportation between South-South and South-East Nigeria.

FEATURED PROJECT

Warri-Effurun Flyover Complex

Contributing to Delta State's N78 billion infrastructure transformation, EDY-BO International supplied construction materials and equipment support for the three flyovers, cloverleaf interchange, and road expansion works in Warri and Effurun metropolis. This project includes PTI Junction, DSC Roundabout, and Enerhen Junction flyovers that are revolutionizing traffic flow in Delta's economic hub.
