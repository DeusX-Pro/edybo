Oil & Gas Infrastructure Excellence

EDY-BO International has been a trusted partner to Nigeria's oil and gas industry since 2006, delivering comprehensive infrastructure solutions across the Niger Delta region. Our specialized services support exploration, production, and distribution operations for major oil companies operating in Delta State's energy corridor.

From the challenging terrain of Escravos to the operational facilities in Warri, we provide critical construction and maintenance services that keep Nigeria's energy sector running efficiently. Our expertise includes pipeline infrastructure, access roads to remote oil fields, platform support structures, and facility maintenance.

We understand the unique demands of oil and gas operations in the Niger Delta – the environmental considerations, safety requirements, and logistical challenges of working in riverine and swamp terrain. Our team combines international safety standards with local knowledge to deliver projects that meet the stringent requirements of this critical industry.

With over 18 years of proven experience, EDY-BO International continues to be the contractor of choice for oil and gas companies seeking reliable, safety-focused, and technically competent construction services in Delta State and across the Niger Delta region.

Wellhead Maintenance & Optimization

EDY-BO International provides comprehensive wellhead maintenance and repair services that ensure optimal performance, safety, and longevity of well infrastructure. Our specialized team understands the critical importance of wellhead integrity to production operations and safety.

With specialized equipment, experienced technicians, and a commitment to zero incidents, EDY-BO International is the trusted partner for oil and gas operators who demand excellence in wellhead maintenance. We help maximize well productivity while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Corrosion Control & Facility Inspection Services

EDY-BO International provides advanced corrosion control and facility inspection services to protect assets and ensure operational integrity. Our comprehensive approach to asset protection combines preventive measures, regular inspections, and timely interventions to extend equipment life and prevent failures.

Our corrosion control services include cathodic protection system design and installation, protective coating application, and corrosion monitoring programs. We work with pipelines, storage tanks, structures, and equipment to implement effective corrosion prevention strategies.

We offer comprehensive NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) inspection services using multiple techniques including ultrasonic testing, radiography, magnetic particle testing, and visual inspection. Our certified inspectors identify potential issues before they become critical failures.

Regular facility inspections and corrosion monitoring are essential for asset integrity management. EDY-BO International provides the expertise and technology needed to detect corrosion early, assess remaining life, and recommend appropriate interventions to protect your investments.

  • Cathodic Protection Systems
  • Protective Coating Application

Instrumentation & Control Systems

EDY-BO International delivers cutting-edge instrumentation and control systems for process optimization and automation. We design, install, commission, and maintain control systems that enhance operational efficiency, improve safety, and provide comprehensive monitoring capabilities.

Our instrumentation services cover the full range of process measurement and control devices – from pressure and temperature sensors to flow meters, level transmitters, and analytical instruments. We ensure accurate measurement and reliable data for process control and optimization.

Beyond installation, we provide comprehensive maintenance services to keep instrumentation and control systems operating reliably. From calibration and troubleshooting to upgrades and modernization, EDY-BO International ensures your control systems perform optimally.
