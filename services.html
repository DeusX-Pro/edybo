OVERVIEW

Industries We Serve

EDY-BO International operates at the intersection of Nigeria's most critical infrastructure sectors. From supporting the oil and gas industry in the Niger Delta to building the roads and complexes that drive economic growth across Delta State, we deliver solutions that power progress.

Our expertise spans oil and gas infrastructure, road construction, industrial facilities, and complex development projects. With over 18 years of experience navigating the unique challenges of the Niger Delta terrain, we combine international best practices with deep local knowledge to deliver projects safely, efficiently, and sustainably.
