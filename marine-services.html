OVERVIEW

Comprehensive Marine Services

EDY-BO International provides comprehensive marine services supporting offshore oil and gas operations throughout the Niger Delta. We ensure safe and efficient transportation of personnel, equipment, and materials to offshore locations through our vessel chartering, crew boat services, and marine logistics capabilities.

Our marine services include crew boat operations, supply vessel chartering, barge services, and specialized marine transport. We work with qualified vessel operators who meet international marine safety standards and maintain properly certified vessels.

Marine safety is our top priority. All vessels we charter undergo rigorous safety audits, and we ensure compliance with maritime regulations and industry standards. Our marine coordination team manages scheduling, routing, and logistics to optimize efficiency while maintaining safety.

Whether you need daily crew boat services to offshore platforms, heavy lift barge operations for equipment installation, or specialized marine support for offshore projects, EDY-BO International delivers reliable marine services that keep offshore operations running smoothly and safely.
