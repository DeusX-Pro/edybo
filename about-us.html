Our values

EDY-BO International (NIG) Limited is a premier construction and oil & gas service provider based in Delta State, Nigeria. Since 2006, we have built a reputation for excellence in delivering complex infrastructure projects across the Niger Delta region. Our core expertise spans oil and gas infrastructure development, road construction, industrial facility construction, and large-scale complex developments. We are committed to safety, environmental sustainability, and creating lasting value for our clients and communities.

Our operations are guided by international best practices while leveraging deep local knowledge of Delta State's unique terrain and regulatory environment. From supporting major oil companies in Escravos to building critical road infrastructure connecting communities across the state, EDY-BO International delivers quality, reliability, and innovation on every project.
